Projects

Techstpe
Crafting a Dynamic Brand for Mobile Tech

The Challenge

Techstep’s rapid growth left its brand identity unclear. My role was to help shape a cohesive brand that would communicate their core values and align with their future goals.

The Insight

Through workshops and competitor analysis, we discovered that Techstep’s strength lay in using mobile tech to empower people, promoting efficient, secure, and flexible work environments.


My UX Approach

I helped redesign Techstep’s brand architecture, creating a fresh visual identity and a microsite for brand assets. I also collaborated with the product team to enhance the customer website and ensure alignment with the new brand.

Work by Ahmed Abdul

Results

The rebrand launched with a series of global presentations, successfully energizing Techstep’s workforce and reinforcing their renewed brand identity.

One of the biggest campaigns we’ve undertaken for the Corporate & Commercial Bank.

Dan Sherwood – Marketing Director at techstep.
